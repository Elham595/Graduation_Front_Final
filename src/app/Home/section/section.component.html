Fashion design provides a community that collect client, fabric stores, designers and tailors all in one place. Provides services for each of them for client : can shop from fabric stores, connect with designers to make a specific design or any design he wants, also connect with tailors and make an order. For designer& tailor& store : can display their designs or works on their page and the site provides a chat service to chat with designer& tailor& store to know more details and make a deal or offer to be ordered.
